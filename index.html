ABOUT THIS VOLUME

Written for the serious aspirant.

This book has been built specifically for the TNPSC Group 1 Geography syllabus. Every chapter is written for slow, careful reading — the kind that builds permanent understanding rather than fleeting memorisation. Each chapter opens with objectives, follows a logical flow, embeds verified facts in callout blocks, and ends with a recap and ten exam-style MCQs with full explanations. TNPSC papers are bilingual, and so key geographical vocabulary appears alongside its Tamil rendering — e.g. Western Ghats (மேற்கு தொடர்ச்சி மலை).

How to use this book Method

  • Read each chapter twice — first quickly for shape, then slowly for depth.
  • Attempt every MCQ before peeking at the answer; that gap is where learning happens.
  • Pair Chapters 3–9 with Chapter 19 (TN physiography) — many TN questions become easy once the all-India picture is firm.
  • Spend the most time on Part IX. It carries the highest exam weight.

What's inside every chapter Structure

  • Objectives — what you'll be able to answer after.
  • Callouts — Key Fact, TNPSC Tip, Tamil Nadu Connection, Mnemonic.
  • Tables & maps — every figure is exam-relevant.
  • Recap + 10 MCQs — the closing rhythm of every chapter.

Sources behind the facts Verified

  • NCERT Class XI & XII Geography textbooks
  • India Yearbook 2024 (Publications Division)
  • Census of India 2011 & subsequent releases
  • IMD, GSI, FSI, MoSPI, Survey of India
  • Tamil Nadu policy notes & statistical handbooks

Section weight in the exam Exam tip

  • Tamil Nadu (Part IX) — the highest-yield section. Master it.
  • Physiography & Drainage (III–IV) — high-frequency factual questions.
  • Agriculture & Industries (VII) — overlaps with Economy paper.
  • World Geography (X) — limited but predictable. Don't skip.
