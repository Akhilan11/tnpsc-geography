# TNPSC Group 1 — Geography Master Volume
## Master Outline & Production Plan

**Target:** ~500 pages, exam-grade depth, bilingual key terms (English + தமிழ்), beautiful light-themed HTML reading experience, publication-quality.

**Primary sources used for verification:**
- NCERT Class XI — *India: Physical Environment*
- NCERT Class XII — *Fundamentals of Human Geography*; *India: People and Economy*
- *India 2024 Yearbook* (Publications Division, Govt. of India)
- Census of India 2011 (releases by Office of the Registrar General)
- Geological Survey of India (GSI) publications
- India Meteorological Department (IMD)
- Tamil Nadu Government Policy Notes (Agriculture, Industries, Statistics)
- Survey of India maps & TN State Atlas
- Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation (MoSPI)
- TNPSC Group 1 official syllabus (latest notification)

**Fact-verification rule:** Every quantitative claim (river length, peak height, area, % share, district count, population figure) is cross-checked against at least one of the above. Any uncertain figure is either omitted or marked with the source year.

## Pedagogical Features (every chapter has these)

1. **Chapter Opener** — Objectives, "Why this matters for TNPSC", time to study
2. **Bilingual key terms** — Tamil rendering for every important term
3. **Maps & SVG diagrams** — generated programmatically, exam-style
4. **Callout boxes:**
   - `Key Fact` — must-memorize numbers
   - `TNPSC Tip` — exam-specific guidance
   - `Tamil Nadu Connection` — local relevance
   - `Mnemonic Box` — memory aids
   - `Compare & Contrast` — differentiation tables
5. **Worked example** — model UPSC/TNPSC question solved
6. **Practice MCQs** — 10 questions per chapter with detailed answers
7. **Quick Recap** — bullet summary at end
8. **Cross-references** — links between chapters
