CHAPTER 30 / PRACTICE MOCK TESTS

Practice Mock Tests.

Sixty cumulative questions in four 15-question mock tests. Time yourself: 15 minutes per test. Treat them as exam-day rehearsals.

HOW TO TAKE THESE MOCKS

  • Each test: 15 questions in 15 minutes.
  • Cover the answers. Pick yours. Reveal only when finished.
  • Aim for 80%+. Anything below 70%, revisit the relevant chapter.
  • Repeat tests 3 days later — track improvement.

Mock Test 1 — 15 Questions

CUMULATIVE · 15 MIN
01Indira Point, India's southernmost point, is located on which island?
  1. Minicoy
  2. South Andaman
  3. Great Nicobar
  4. Barren Island
Reveal

C — Great Nicobar. (6° 45′ N)

02"Sahyadri" is the local name for the —
  1. Eastern Ghats
  2. Western Ghats
  3. Aravallis
  4. Satpuras
Reveal

B — Western Ghats.

03Which is the only state through which the Indus river system flows in India?
  1. Punjab
  2. Himachal Pradesh
  3. Ladakh (UT) + J&K + Punjab + HP
  4. J&K only
Reveal

C — Multiple. Indus main stem flows through Ladakh; tributaries cover J&K, HP, Punjab.

04The "rain shadow" effect within India is most strongly seen on which side of the Western Ghats?
  1. Western (windward)
  2. Eastern (leeward)
  3. Northern
  4. Southern
Reveal

B — Eastern (leeward) — the Deccan Plateau lies in rain shadow.

05Madurai is famous for its temple dedicated to —
  1. Vishnu
  2. Shiva
  3. Meenakshi-Sundareswarar
  4. Murugan
Reveal

C — Meenakshi-Sundareswarar Amman temple.

06The largest delta-building river system of India is —
  1. Krishna-Godavari
  2. Ganga-Brahmaputra
  3. Mahanadi
  4. Cauvery
Reveal

B — Ganga-Brahmaputra (Sundarbans).

07The Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is in —
  1. Tamil Nadu
  2. Karnataka
  3. Kerala
  4. Andhra Pradesh
Reveal

C — Kerala (Idukki district).

08The Trans-Himalayas include —
  1. Himadri only
  2. Pir Panjal
  3. Karakoram, Ladakh, Zanskar
  4. Shivaliks
Reveal

C — Karakoram, Ladakh, Zanskar (Kailash optional).

09Which Indian state has the highest forest cover percentage?
  1. Madhya Pradesh
  2. Arunachal Pradesh
  3. Mizoram
  4. Chhattisgarh
Reveal

C — Mizoram (~ 84%+, ISFR).

10The largest producer of pulses in the world is —
  1. USA
  2. Canada
  3. India
  4. Brazil
Reveal

C — India.

11Tamil Nadu's coastline is approximately —
  1. 906 km
  2. 1,076 km
  3. 1,214 km
  4. 1,440 km
Reveal

B — 1,076 km.

12The Naharkatiya-Barauni pipeline was India's first —
  1. Gas pipeline
  2. Crude oil pipeline
  3. Water pipeline
  4. Petrochemical pipeline
Reveal

B — Crude oil pipeline (1962).

13The Loktak Lake is in —
  1. Mizoram
  2. Manipur
  3. Nagaland
  4. Assam
Reveal

B — Manipur. Hosts Keibul Lamjao NP.

14The first all-India census was conducted in —
  1. 1872
  2. 1881
  3. 1891
  4. 1901
Reveal

B — 1881 (synchronously). 1872 was partial.

15Which is the largest tribe in India by population?
  1. Bhil
  2. Santhal
  3. Gond
  4. Naga
Reveal

C — Gond.

Mock Test 2 — 15 Questions

CUMULATIVE · 15 MIN
16"Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary" is in —
  1. Goa
  2. Maharashtra
  3. Kerala
  4. Karnataka
Reveal

A — Goa (Chorao Island).

17The Konkan coast lies along the coast of —
  1. Maharashtra + Goa
  2. Kerala
  3. Karnataka
  4. Tamil Nadu
Reveal

A — Maharashtra & Goa.

18India's first satellite was —
  1. Bhaskara
  2. Rohini
  3. Aryabhata
  4. INSAT-1A
Reveal

C — Aryabhata (April 1975, launched by USSR).

19Which is India's longest railway platform?
  1. Kharagpur
  2. Sonpur
  3. Hubballi (Hubli)
  4. Howrah
Reveal

C — Hubballi (Hubli) — Shri Siddharoodha Swamiji station; 1,507 m, the world's longest railway platform (2023).

20The 'Black Hills' of India are in —
  1. Madhya Pradesh
  2. Karnataka
  3. Tamil Nadu (parts of EG)
  4. Uttarakhand
Reveal

C — Black Hills (Karikkalmalai) in TN — small range in Erode.

21"Operation Flood" was led by —
  1. M. S. Swaminathan
  2. Verghese Kurien
  3. Vikram Sarabhai
  4. Norman Borlaug
Reveal

B — Verghese Kurien (NDDB, Amul).

22The Aravalli range is the source of which of the following rivers?
  1. Yamuna
  2. Banas, Luni, Sabarmati
  3. Mahanadi
  4. Tapi
Reveal

B — Banas, Luni, Sabarmati (and Mahi).

23India's first IT park is in —
  1. Bengaluru
  2. Hyderabad
  3. Chennai
  4. Pune
Reveal

A — Bengaluru (Electronics City, 1978; ITPB 1991-94).

24The Bandipur National Park is in which state?
  1. Tamil Nadu
  2. Karnataka
  3. Kerala
  4. Andhra Pradesh
Reveal

B — Karnataka (adjacent to TN's Mudumalai).

25"Yenadi", "Irula", and "Toda" are tribes principally found in —
  1. Andhra Pradesh / Tamil Nadu
  2. Kerala
  3. Karnataka
  4. Odisha
Reveal

A — Andhra Pradesh / Tamil Nadu (the Nilgiris region especially for Toda).

26Which state has the highest sex ratio in India?
  1. Tamil Nadu
  2. Andhra Pradesh
  3. Kerala
  4. Karnataka
Reveal

C — Kerala (1,084).

27The Tibetan Plateau is called the —
  1. Roof of the World
  2. Roof of Asia
  3. Heart of Asia
  4. Cradle of Civilisation
Reveal

A — Roof of the World (~ 4,500 m avg elevation).

28"Beas" originates near —
  1. Bara Lacha pass
  2. Rohtang pass area
  3. Mansarovar
  4. Karakoram
Reveal

B — Beas Kund, near Rohtang pass (HP).

29"Athirappilly Falls" in Kerala, called the "Niagara of India", is on the —
  1. Periyar
  2. Chalakudy
  3. Pamba
  4. Bharatappuzha
Reveal

B — Chalakudy river.

30The Vindhya range marks the southern boundary of —
  1. The Northern Plain
  2. The Central Highlands
  3. The Deccan Plateau
  4. The Eastern Plateau
Reveal

B — Central Highlands.

Mock Test 3 — 15 Questions

CUMULATIVE · 15 MIN
31"Indira Col" — India's northernmost point — is in —
  1. Sikkim
  2. Arunachal Pradesh
  3. Ladakh (Karakoram)
  4. Himachal Pradesh
Reveal

C — Ladakh / Karakoram.

32The Hirakud dam is built across which river?
  1. Krishna
  2. Mahanadi
  3. Godavari
  4. Cauvery
Reveal

B — Mahanadi (Odisha).

33The Cauvery Water Management Authority (CWMA) was constituted in —
  1. 2007
  2. 2010
  3. 2018
  4. 2021
Reveal

C — 2018.

34"NW-3" (National Waterway 3) runs along —
  1. Ganga
  2. Brahmaputra
  3. West Coast Canal (Kerala)
  4. Krishna
Reveal

C — West Coast Canal, Kerala.

35The "Pearl City of India" is —
  1. Chennai
  2. Cochin
  3. Thoothukudi
  4. Mumbai
Reveal

C — Thoothukudi (Tuticorin), TN.

36The Kalpakkam atomic site hosts —
  1. VVER reactors
  2. Fast Breeder Reactor (PFBR) & PHWR
  3. Boiling water reactors only
  4. Pressurised water reactors only
Reveal

B — PFBR + PHWR.

37"Kovalam" beach is in —
  1. Kerala
  2. Tamil Nadu
  3. Both A and B (there are two Kovalam beaches)
  4. Karnataka
Reveal

C — Both. The famous Kovalam is in Thiruvananthapuram (Kerala); there's also a Kovalam near Chennai in TN.

38The Aliyar Dam is on the —
  1. Cauvery
  2. Bhavani
  3. Aliyar (tributary of Amaravathi)
  4. Noyyal
Reveal

C — Aliyar river.

39The "Ten Degree Channel" lies between —
  1. Andaman & Nicobar
  2. India & Sri Lanka
  3. India & Maldives
  4. Andaman & Myanmar
Reveal

A — Andaman & Nicobar.

40India's largest single solar park (Bhadla) is in —
  1. Gujarat
  2. Tamil Nadu
  3. Rajasthan
  4. Andhra Pradesh
Reveal

C — Rajasthan (Jodhpur).

41"Vetti Vellu" (Sangam-era cultural fields) are now called —
  1. Mandalams
  2. Tiṇai
  3. Naadu
  4. Kottam
Reveal

B — Tiṇai.

42The Mullaperiyar Dam is operated by —
  1. Kerala
  2. Tamil Nadu
  3. Central Govt
  4. Karnataka
Reveal

B — Tamil Nadu, under a 999-year lease (1886).

43Salem is famous for which mineral?
  1. Iron ore
  2. Magnesite
  3. Bauxite
  4. All of the above
Reveal

D — All of the above. Plus stainless steel plant.

44"Adam's Bridge" lies between —
  1. Rameswaram and Mannar
  2. Kanyakumari and Sri Lanka
  3. Andaman and Nicobar
  4. Lakshadweep and Maldives
Reveal

A — Rameswaram (Dhanushkodi) to Mannar.

45The "Cyclone Gaja" of 2018 hit Tamil Nadu's —
  1. Chennai metro area
  2. Kanyakumari coast
  3. Cauvery delta (Nagapattinam etc.)
  4. Western Ghats
Reveal

C — Cauvery delta. Devastated coconut plantations.

CUMULATIVE · 15 MIN

  1. 1980
  2. 1985
  3. 1995
  4. 2014

B — 1985 (Phase I). Namami Gange Mission launched 2014.

47The "Three Gorges Dam" (world's largest) is built on which river?
  1. Yangtze
  2. Yellow River
  3. Mekong
  4. Amazon
Reveal

A — Yangtze (China).

48India became the 4th country in the world to have a registered Indigenous AI Mission. Which space mission landed at the Moon's south pole?
  1. Chandrayaan-1
  2. Chandrayaan-2
  3. Chandrayaan-3
  4. Mangalyaan
Reveal

C — Chandrayaan-3 (23 Aug 2023, Vikram lander, Pragyan rover).

49"Bharatmala" project relates to —
  1. Inland waterways
  2. National Highways & expressways
  3. Railways
  4. Telecom
Reveal

B — National Highways & expressways. Launched 2017.

50The Niti Pass connects —
  1. HP to Tibet
  2. Uttarakhand to Tibet
  3. Sikkim to Tibet
  4. Arunachal to China
Reveal

B — Uttarakhand to Tibet.

51The state with the longest border with Bangladesh is —
  1. West Bengal
  2. Assam
  3. Tripura
  4. Meghalaya
Reveal

A — West Bengal (~ 2,217 km of the 4,096 km Bangladesh border).

52"Eight Degree Channel" separates —
  1. India & Sri Lanka
  2. Minicoy & Maldives
  3. Andaman & Nicobar
  4. Minicoy & rest of Lakshadweep
Reveal

B — Minicoy (Lakshadweep) & Maldives.

53India's first refinery (Digboi, Assam) was set up in —
  1. 1854
  2. 1889
  3. 1904
  4. 1947
Reveal

B — 1889.

54"Project Cheetah" reintroduced cheetahs into India in —
  1. Gir, Gujarat
  2. Bandhavgarh, MP
  3. Kuno, MP
  4. Ranthambore, RJ
Reveal

C — Kuno NP, Madhya Pradesh (2022).

55"Auroville" is in —
  1. Tamil Nadu
  2. Puducherry
  3. Pondicherry & TN border
  4. Kerala
Reveal

C — Pondicherry & TN border (mostly Villupuram district of TN).

56India's "Atal Tunnel" connects —
  1. Srinagar & Leh
  2. Manali & Lahaul-Spiti
  3. Delhi & Shimla
  4. Dehradun & Mussoorie
Reveal

B — Manali & Lahaul-Spiti (under Rohtang Pass). World's longest highway tunnel above 10,000 ft.

57"Indravati" is a tributary of —
  1. Krishna
  2. Godavari
  3. Mahanadi
  4. Narmada
Reveal

B — Godavari.

58The "Mawsynram" wettest-place title for Cherrapunji is now held by —
  1. Mawsynram itself (took over from Cherrapunji)
  2. Agumbe, Karnataka
  3. Mahabaleshwar, MH
  4. Pasighat, Arunachal
Reveal

A — Mawsynram (in the Khasi Hills, ~ 11,872 mm/year on average).

59"Sariska Tiger Reserve" is in —
  1. MP
  2. Rajasthan
  3. Gujarat
  4. UP
Reveal

B — Rajasthan (Alwar).
